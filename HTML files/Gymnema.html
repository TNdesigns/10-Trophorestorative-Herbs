Gymnema(Gymnema Sylvestre)

Organ: Pancreas

Pharmacological properties:
  1. Arthritis
  2. Diuretic
  3. Anemia
  4. Osteoporosis
  5. Hypercholesterolemia
  6. Cardiopathy
  7. Asthma
  8. Constipation
  9. Indigestion
  10. Anti-inflammatory

Gymnema has a trophorestorative action of the beta cells of the pancreas. The key constituents of Gymnema include saponins and gymnemic acids. Key constituents suppress the perception of the sweet taste on the taste buds, so if taken before food, masks sweet detection and reduces desire for sweet foods and caloric intake for 90 minutes after administration. Gymnema regenerates the endocrine cells of the pancreas and enhances insulin secretion. Gymnema prevents the absorption of excess glucose in the small intestine, thereby helping to balance the amount of glucose in your bloodstream. Gymnema also helps the pancreas to regenerate islets of Langerhans that secrete inslin and glucagon. The herbal extract of Gymnema reduces body weight, blood cholesterol, and triglyceride levels and holds great prospects in dietary as well as pharmacological applications. Gymnema sylvestre supplementation restores normoglycemia, and corrects dyslipidemia.
